About Our Brand

Founded by Tisha with a passion for sustainable fashion and timeless style

Our Story

Tisha's Clothing started as a small venture in 2018, born from a desire to create garments that respect traditional craftsmanship while embracing contemporary designs.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed and ethically produced, using sustainable materials and supporting local artisans.

Our commitment to quality and attention to detail ensures that every item not only looks exceptional but stands the test of time.
